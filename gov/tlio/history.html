History of TLIO

How It All Began

The Thelol Intelligence Organization (TLIO) was founded by Faiz4224 in mid-2023 as an initiative to prevent the division of The LoL. Originally named the League of TheLoL Traitors Association, this organization played a pivotal role in the mission to remove TheLoL II President, UltraX2020, who promoted Communist and Capitalist ideologies.

The Transformation

After the successful mission, the League of TheLoL Traitors Association was restructured into the Thelol Intelligence Organization (TLIO) on November 8, 2023. The organization now functions as a key intelligence body responsible for maintaining the security of The LoL territories.

TLIO's Role Today

TLIO addresses a wide range of issues, including crime prevention, conflict resolution, crisis management, and wartime operations. It stands as a symbol of unity and strategic brilliance, ensuring peace and stability within The LoL.
