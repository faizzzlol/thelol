The LoL Proclamation of Sovereignty
"In the Name of Unity, Legacy, and Glory"

We, the Founders and People of The Legend of Legiona, hereby proclaim to all realms — from the Overworld to the Nether and the End — the establishment of a sovereign and unified nation forged by vision, courage, and camaraderie.

This Declaration marks the dawn of an era where the values of justice, prosperity, and fellowship reign supreme.

The Legend of Legiona (formerly known as The Sus) was founded through the combined efforts and dreams of its three visionary Founders.

National History

OFFICIAL RECORD
2023
EARLY

The Founding — "The Sus"

The LoL was originally called "The Sus", founded by Faiz4224, ItzDynozz, and Imii Kun. Small but visionary, the three founders laid the groundwork for what would become the most powerful nation in Skyxion.

FOUNDING ERA
2023

Renaming & Expansion — The Legend of Legiona

The nation was renamed "The Legend of Legiona" and rapidly became the most modern and wealthiest nation in Skyxion. Under President Faiz4224, The LoL thrived in economy, infrastructure, and innovation.

EXPANSION
MAY 6
2023

First Presidential Election

The LoL held its first democratic election. UltraX2020 and the PHRTL party won, becoming the 2nd President of The LoL — marking a significant turning point in its governance.

DEMOCRATIC MILESTONE
2023
CRISIS

UltraX2020 Presidency — Division & Chaos

Internal strife surfaced under UltraX2020's rule. The LoL City sign was bombed, TLCC was attacked by drones, and political conflicts gripped the nation. UltraX2020 eventually resigned, returning power to Faiz4224.

CONFLICT ERA
NOV 8
2023

Post-Chaos Recovery & TLIO Formation

Faiz4224 resumed leadership. Infrastructure projects launched, security reinforced. The intelligence body TLIO was established to defend The LoL from future threats and preserve national stability.

REBUILDING
2025–
2026

Modern Era — ISC & Skyxion: Ragnarok

TLIO was reborn as the Internal Security Control (ISC) — a formal, CIA-inspired agency. The LoL stands today as a symbol of perseverance and innovation in the Skyxion: Ragnarok era.
