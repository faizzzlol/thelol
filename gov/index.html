The LoL Proclamation of Sovereignty
"In the Name of Unity, Legacy, and Glory"

We, the Founders and People of The Legend of Legiona, hereby proclaim to all realms — from the Overworld to the Nether and the End — the establishment of a sovereign and unified nation forged by vision, courage, and camaraderie.

This Declaration marks the dawn of an era where the values of justice, prosperity, and fellowship reign supreme. The Legend of Legiona (formerly known as The Sus) was founded through the combined efforts and dreams of its three visionary Founders.

Our Founding Principles

As decreed in the original Proclamation, The Legend of Legiona is built upon four enduring pillars that guide every act of governance and citizenship.

Unity

Where all players — builders, warriors, and adventurers — stand as one, striving for collective greatness.

National History

2023 — Early
Founding Era

The Founding — "The Sus"

The LoL was originally called "The Sus", founded by Faiz4224, ItzDynozz, and Imii Kun. Small but visionary, the three founders laid the groundwork for what would become the most powerful nation in Skyxion. During this era, EhekSquad — led by PhoenixAiman, PandaPutih, and Kagee — joined The Sus, though their exact status as citizens, allies, or simply residents was never formally defined, as The Sus had no policy or governance structure. EhekSquad later departed and re-established independently.

2023
Expansion

Renaming & Expansion — The Legend of Legiona

The nation was renamed "The Legend of Legiona" and rapidly became the most modern and wealthiest nation in Skyxion. Under President Faiz4224, The LoL thrived in economy, infrastructure, and innovation.

May 6, 2023
Democratic Milestone

First Presidential Election

The LoL held its first democratic election. UltraX2020 and the Parti Harapan Rakyat The LoL (PHRTL) party won, becoming the 2nd President of The LoL — marking a significant turning point in its governance.

2023 — Crisis
Conflict Era

UltraX2020 Presidency — Division & Chaos

Internal strife surfaced under UltraX2020's rule. The LoL City sign was bombed, TLCC was attacked by drones, and political conflicts gripped the nation. UltraX2020 eventually resigned, returning power to Faiz4224.

Nov 8, 2023
Rebuilding

Post-Chaos Recovery & TLIO Formation

Faiz4224 resumed leadership. Infrastructure projects launched, security reinforced. The intelligence body TLIO (now known as ISC) was established to defend The LoL from future threats and preserve national stability.

2025–2026
Current Era

Modern Era — ISC & Skyxion: Altaer

TLIO was reborn as the Internal Security Control (ISC) — a formal, intelligence agency. The LoL stands today as a symbol of perseverance and innovation under overworld, in the Skyxion: Altaer era.
