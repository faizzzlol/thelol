Sovereignty & Unity

Building upon the original Declaration of Establishment, the ISC continues to uphold the core principle that the Legiona is a sovereign entity. As originally decreed, our existence is a "testament to the strength of a united community."


The ISC remains the primary executor of the mandate to protect our digital and social borders from external destabilization.
