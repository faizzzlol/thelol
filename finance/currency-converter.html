How to use?

Step 1: Choose the Conversion Direction

1. Convert from: Use the first dropdown menu labeled "Convert from" to select the currency you want to convert from. Options include Gold Ingot, Iron, Emerald, Diamond, Netherite, and TL$ (TL$ stands for The LoL Dollar).

2. Amount: Enter the amount of the selected currency you want to convert in the "Amount" input field.

Step 2: Choose the Target Currency

1. Convert to: Use the second dropdown menu labeled "Convert to" to select the currency you want to convert to. Again, the options include Gold Ingot, Iron, Emerald, Diamond, Netherite, and TL$.

Step 3: Perform the Conversion

1. After selecting the source currency, entering the amount, and choosing the target currency, click the "Convert" button. The webpage will display the converted amount in the selected target currency.

Example Scenario:

• If you want to convert 10 Gold Ingots to TL$, you would select "Gold Ingot" in the "Convert from" menu, enter "10" in the "Amount" field, select "TL$" in the "Convert to" menu, and then click "Convert." The result will show you how many TL$ you get for 10 Gold Ingots.

Notes:

• The conversion rates are predefined: 1 Gold Ingots = 4 TL$, 1 Iron Ingots = 0 TL$, 1 Emeralds = 10 TL$, 1 Diamonds = 30 TL$, 1 Netherite Ingots = 50 TL$.

• The "TL$" currency can also be converted back to any of the Minecraft currencies using the same process.

Step 4: Additional Features

1. If you want to switch the direction of the conversion (e.g., convert from TL$ to Gold Ingot), you can use the dropdown menus to choose the desired conversion direction.

Step 5: Explore Other Sections

Feel free to explore other sections of the webpage, such as downloading texture packs or accessing additional information about our Minecraft server.
