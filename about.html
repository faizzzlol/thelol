About The Legend Of Legiona

Our Mission

The Legend Of Legiona (The LoL) is a creative community and Minecraft server built to bring players together in a unique and immersive world. We strive to create a one-of-a-kind gaming experience, where players can express their creativity, participate in events, and enjoy a dynamic and ever-evolving virtual world.

History

The Legend Of Legiona (originally named "The Sus") was founded by Faiz4224 with ItzDynozz and Imii kun. Later, the name was changed to The Legend of Legiona (The LoL), and it became the most modern and rich nation in Skyxion, ruled by President Faiz4224. The first election was held on 06/05/2023, where Parti Perjuangan Rakyat The LoL (PPRTL) led by Faiz4224 was defeated by Parti Pakatan Harapan Rakyat The LoL (PHRTL), led by UltraX2020.

UltraX2020 became the 2nd President of The LoL, but during their leadership, The LoL faced several divisions and chaotic events. The LoL city sign was bombed, TLCC was attacked by a drone, and there was widespread turmoil. Despite these challenges, UltraX2020 eventually resigned as President, returning full presidential power to Faiz4224, who continues to lead The LoL today.

Meet the Team

The LoL is led by a group of dedicated individuals who are passionate about Minecraft and server development. Our team works around the clock to ensure that players have a fun and smooth experience, with regular updates and community events.
