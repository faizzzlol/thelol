CHAPTER 01

The Founding

ORIGINAL NAME: THE SUS

The LoL was originally called "The Sus", founded by Faiz4224, alongside ItzDynozz and Imii Kun. During its inception, the nation was small, but the collaboration between these three founders laid the groundwork for future developments. EhekSquad — led by PhoenixAiman, PandaPutih, and Kagee — also joined during this era, though their exact status was never formally defined as The Sus had no policy or governance structure. They later departed and re-established EhekSquad independently.

ORIGIN ERA
2023
CHAPTER 02

Renaming & Expansion

THE LEGEND OF LEGIONA IS BORN

As the nation grew, the founders decided to rename it "The Legend of Legiona", abbreviated as The LoL. The LoL rapidly transformed into the most modern and wealthiest nation in Skyxion — a region known for its diverse cultures and nations. Under President Faiz4224, The LoL thrived in economy, infrastructure, and innovation.

GOLDEN ERA
MAY 6
2023
CHAPTER 03

The First Presidential Election

ELECTION ANNOUNCEMENT

On May 6, 2023, The LoL held its first democratic election — marking a significant turning point in its governance. Despite Faiz4224's strong leadership, UltraX2020 and the PHRTL party won the election, making UltraX2020 the 2nd President of The LoL.

DEMOCRATIC MILESTONE
2023
CRISIS
CHAPTER 04

UltraX2020's Presidency

DIVISION AND CHAOS

Under UltraX2020's rule, internal strife and divisions surfaced. The iconic LoL City sign was bombed, TLCC was attacked by drones, and political conflicts gripped the city. The nation's stability was tested like never before.

CONFLICT ERA
2023
LATE
CHAPTER 05

The Resignation of UltraX2020

LEADERSHIP CRISIS AND RESIGNATION

UltraX2020 resigned from the presidency following the chaos. He returned full power back to Faiz4224, restoring peace to The LoL. Faiz4224 continued to lead with a renewed vision of progress and unity.

RESTORATION
NOV 8
2023
CHAPTER 06

Post-Chaos Recovery

REBUILDING AND MOVING FORWARD

After the political turmoil, The LoL began a rebuilding phase. Faiz4224's government initiated several infrastructure projects, improved security with the founding of TLIO (later reborn as ISC), and focused on uniting the nation under one banner once again. The Black House — The LoL's presidential office — was established as the seat of executive governance.

CHAPTER 07

The Modern Era

SKYXION: ALTAER ERA // LOOKING AHEAD

Today, The LoL stands as a symbol of perseverance and innovation. TLIO has been reborn as ISC (Internal Security Control). Under Skyxion : Altaer era, The LoL continues to operate with full sovereignty, the Landen Accord, and an active egalitarian governance model. The LoL is poised to continue its legacy as one of the most modern and successful nations in Skyxion.
