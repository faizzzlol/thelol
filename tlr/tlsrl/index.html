The Railway Itself

Every block of the TLSRL track was laid by Paiz™ Construction — the engineering arm of Paiz® Corp. Spanning 4,800+ blocks, the enclosed track design protects passengers and cargo from mob interference and environmental obstacles throughout the entire route.

Each of the 6 stations along the TLSRL route was designed and constructed to serve its specific role — from the grand terminus facilities at The LoL and Spawn, to the interchange architecture at LoL City Central and Skyxion Junction.

ORIGIN

Faiz4224's Vision

The TLSRL was conceived and proposed by Faiz4224, First President of The Legend of Legiona. The project was driven by a vision to give The LoL a permanent, high-quality link to Skyxion Spawn — cementing The LoL's place in the server's infrastructure landscape.

Operational Excellence

TL Railways operates all rolling stock and system management across the TLSRL route. Three distinct service tiers — Regular, Express, and Cargo — are maintained daily, with operational oversight provided by TL Railways' network control.
