Founding Document
The LoL Proclamation of Sovereignty
"In the Name of Unity, Legacy, and Glory"

We, the Founders and People of The Legend of Legiona, hereby proclaim to all realms — from the Overworld to the Nether and the End — the establishment of a sovereign and unified nation forged by vision, courage, and camaraderie.

This Declaration marks the dawn of an era where the values of justice, prosperity, and fellowship reign supreme. The Legend of Legiona (formerly known as The Sus) was founded through the combined efforts and dreams of its three visionary Founders.

Faiz4224
First President & Founding Leader
Imii Kun
Co-Founder & Visionary
Dyno
Co-Founder & Strategist

Our Founding Principles

As decreed in the original Proclamation, The Legend of Legiona is built upon four enduring pillars that guide every act of governance and citizenship.

01 // PILLAR

Unity

Where all players — builders, warriors, and adventurers — stand as one, striving for collective greatness.

02 // PILLAR

Freedom

Ensuring the right of every citizen to explore, create, and thrive within our borders.

03 // PILLAR

Innovation

Harnessing the limitless possibilities of the Minecraft world to build a nation unlike any other.

04 // PILLAR

Defence & Honour

Protecting our lands from griefers, raiders, and threats while holding true to our principles of fairness.
