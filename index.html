THE LEGEND OF LEGIONA

More than a nation —
a legacy.

The Legend of Legiona, known simply as The LoL, is a sovereign nation within the Skyxion world. Originally founded as a small squad called "The Sus" by Faiz4224, ItzDynozz, and Imii Kun, the nation rapidly grew into the most modern and wealthiest in all of Skyxion.

Through democratic elections, political conflict, a presidential resistance, and full recovery — The LoL has proven that unity and perseverance can overcome anything. Today, it stands as a symbol of resilience, innovation, and sovereign pride.

"In the Name of Unity, Legacy, and Glory." — Proclamation of Sovereignty
