ABOUT THE FOUNDER
The mind behind Paiz® Corp.

Faiz4224 founded Paiz® Corp as The LoL's answer to one question: how do you build a self-sufficient sovereign nation from nothing? The answer was a national conglomerate spanning every sector the nation needs — transport, construction, retail, entertainment, and food.

As the First President of The Legend of Legiona, Faiz4224 proposed the TLSRL railway, commissioned the TLCC Twin Towers, established TL Railways, launched PaizShop, greenlit The LoL Movie through PaizProductions, and opened Paiz Chicken. Each company exists to serve The LoL's citizens.
