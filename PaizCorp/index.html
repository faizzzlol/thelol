CORPORATE HISTORY

How We Grew.

SKYXION: ALTAER
THE LOL TERRITORY
2023

Foundation of Paiz® Corp

Faiz4224 establishes Paiz® Corp as the national conglomerate of The Legend of Legiona. Paiz™ Construction and TL Railways created as the first two subsidiaries.

2023

TLSRL Construction Begins

Paiz™ Construction begins work on the 4,800-block TLSRL railway — The LoL's most ambitious infrastructure project. Six stations. Three service classes. First major build under the Paiz® Corp banner.

2024

Paiz Shop & Paiz Chicken Launch

The retail and food divisions launch — expanding Paiz® Corp beyond infrastructure into commerce and hospitality. Paiz Shop opens with Diamond-priced items; Paiz Chicken launches virtual food ordering with delivery across Skyxion.

2025

PaizProductions Established

The fifth subsidiary is created. PaizProductions begins production on The LoL: The Movie — a four-phase political Minecraft epic. LoL Chronicles and ISC: Classified also enter development and planning.

2026

Conglomerate Expansion & Full Web Ecosystem

All five subsidiaries fully operational. Paiz Shop upgraded with Discord webhook ordering and GitHub-powered admin system. Full web ecosystem live across 6 interconnected sites. Paiz® Corp enters its most ambitious phase yet.

ABOUT THE FOUNDER
The mind behind Paiz® Corp.

Faiz4224 founded Paiz® Corp as The LoL's answer to one question: how do you build a self-sufficient sovereign nation from nothing? The answer — a national conglomerate spanning every sector the nation needs.

As First President of The Legend of Legiona, Faiz4224 proposed the TLSRL railway, commissioned the TLCC Twin Towers, established TL Railways, launched PaizShop, greenlit The LoL Movie through PaizProductions, and opened Paiz Chicken. Every company exists to serve The LoL's citizens.
