PROJECT OVERVIEW

About TLSRL

MEGAPROJECT-01
PAIZ® CORP PORTFOLIO

The The LoL–Spawn Railway Link (TLSRL) is the crown jewel of Paiz® Corp's infrastructure portfolio and The LoL's most ambitious national project. Proposed by Faiz4224 and engineered by Paiz™ Construction, TLSRL spans over 4,800 blocks of continuous track through the landscapes of Skyxion.

The project was conceived to solve The LoL's most critical infrastructure gap — connecting the nation directly to Spawn, Skyxion's central hub. Prior to TLSRL, citizens had no reliable, fast transport link to the wider world. Today, the journey takes just ~10 minutes end-to-end.

Operated by TL Railways (a Paiz® Corp subsidiary), TLSRL runs three service tiers — Regular, Express, and Cargo — with station facilities including ender chest storage and escalator access at major interchanges. It remains the longest and most complex rail project ever undertaken in The LoL's history.
